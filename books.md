# Books Worth Reading

## Fiction

Great fiction books that have shaped my thinking.

### The Ministry for the Future
- Author: Kim Stanley Robinson
- ISBN: 9780316300131
- Image: https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/S/compressed.photo.goodreads.com/books/1606853717i/50998056.jpg
- Links:
  - [Libro.fm](https://libro.fm/audiobooks/9780316300131)
- Note: A powerful cli-fi novel that explores solutions to climate change through near-future scenarios.

### Parable of the Sower
- Author: Octavia Butler
- ISBN: 9781538732182
- Image: https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/S/compressed.photo.goodreads.com/books/1442169447i/52397.jpg
- Note: A prophetic dystopian novel about resilience and community in the face of societal collapse.

## Non-Fiction

Books about technology, society, and the future.
